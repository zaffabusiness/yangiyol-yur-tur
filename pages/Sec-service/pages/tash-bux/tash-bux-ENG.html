Day 1: Tashkent

Arrival at 7:40 by flight TK368 from Istanbul. on Saturday 23 October

Meeting from Islam Karimov International Airport. Hotel accommodation

Early check-in, breakfast, rest, then the start of the tour

You will visit the Old City, where the religious center of Tashkent complex Khast-Imam is located. It is here that the famous Koran of Caliph Osman (VII century) is kept. In Khast-Imam you will visit the Barak-Khan Madrassah, the Tilla-Sheikh Mosque, the Abu Bakr Kaffal Shashi Mausoleum and the Islamic Institute named after Imam al-Bukhari. Then you will visit one of the oldest bazaars of the city - Chorsu. After sightseeing the Old City, you will have a trip to the center where Amir Timur Square, Independence Square and the Museum of Applied Arts are located.

19:00: Dinner at the restaurant "Bukhara"

Day 2: Tashkent – Samarkand

Transfer from the hotel to the station. The high-speed train "Afrosiab" departs from Tashkent at 08:00. During the journey, you will have the opportunity to taste the exquisite delicacies of oriental cuisine and enjoy Uzbek wine. The train arrives at 10:10 in Samarkand. Here you will be met by a guide and a driver who will accompany you during the tour. You will go to the famous Registan Square, then visit the Bibi-Khanum mosque.

13:00: Dinner time

Visiting the theater of national costume "El-Merosi" where you can get acquainted with the original culture of the people of Uzbekistan

17:00:Free time/shopping in the old town

19:00: lunch at the restaurant "Zafar". Overnight at the hotel.

Day 3: Samarkand

You will also visit the ensemble of mausoleums of the Samarkand nobility Shakhi-Zinda and the Gur-Emir mausoleum, the tomb of Amir Timur and the Timurids. Then you will go to the village of Konigil where you will see the process of making Samarkand paper from mulberry using ancient technology.

13:00: Dinner time

A trip to the "Meros" silk factory, where silk carpets, which are famous all over the world, are knitted in the traditional way.

17:00: Free time/shopping in the old town.

19:00: Lunch at the restaurant "Karimbek" Overnight at the hotel

Day 4: Samarkand – Bukhara

09:30: Departure from Samarkand to Bukhara by high-speed train "Afrasiyab" (280 km., 4 h.)

12:00 arrival in Bukhara, hotel accommodation.

13:00: Dinner time

Tour of the Old City, listed in the UNESCO World Heritage List. Visit Kalyan minaret, Kalyan mosque, Miri-Arab madrasah, Magoki-Attori mosque, Ulugbek madrasah, Abdulaziz Khan madrasah, trade domes, Lyabi-Khauz complex, Chor-Minor madrasah.

Overnight at the hotel.

Day-5: Bukhara

Then you will visit the Bolo-Khauz mosque, the Samanid mausoleum and the Chashma-Ayub mausoleum. After excursions in Bukhara, you will have a trip out of town to the summer residence of the last Bukhara emir, Sitorai Mokhi-Khosa. This is a unique building whose architecture combines oriental and Russian decor elements. Continuation of the excursion in the mausoleum of Bakhouddin Nakshbandi

13:00: Dinner time

14:30: After lunch, free time in the old town for shopping.

Overnight at the hotel.

PAY 6: Bukhara-Khiva

The Great Silk Road, which once passed through Bukhara and Khiva, ran through the desert, this is what the current road does. Stop along the way for views of the desert and the Amu Darya River

13:00: Lunch at a local cafe

Walk through the old town

Overnight at the hotel.

Day-7: Khiva - Urgench

The tour includes a visit to the madrasah of Muhammad Amin Khan, the madrasah of Muhammad Rakhimkhan, Malaya Kalta, the Juma mosque, the Toshkhovli palace, the mausoleum of Pakhlavan Mahmud. Try your hand at climbing the Islam Khoja Minaret, the highest minaret in Uzbekistan. Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of Old Khiva in Kunya Arka

13:00: Lunch at a local cafe

Walk through the old town

Overnight at the hotel.

Therefore, we propose to spend the night either in Khiva or in Urgench

Day-8: Urgench - Tashkent

In the morning you will be taken to Urgench airport

9:35 departure from Urgench

10:55 arrival in Tashkent

Overnight at the hotel.

Departure from the hotel. Transfer to the international airport of Tashkent. End of tour. departure at 9:40 by flight TK 369 to Istanbul
